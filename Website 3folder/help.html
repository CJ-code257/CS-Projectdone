Volunteer Opportunities

Our volunteers are the heart of EqualEd. We offer flexible opportunities that fit your schedule and skills:

  • Academic Tutoring: Help students with homework, test preparation, and subject-specific learning (Math, English, Science)
  • Technology Training: Teach digital literacy skills to students and teachers in underserved communities
  • Mobile Learning Center Staff: Travel to remote areas to deliver educational programs and resources
  • Mentorship Programs: Guide students through personal development and career planning
  • Administrative Support: Assist with organizing events, managing programs, and coordinating volunteers
  • Content Creation: Develop educational materials, videos, and resources for our programs

Corporate Partnerships

Organizations and businesses can multiply their impact through strategic partnerships with EqualEd. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Employee Volunteer Programs: Organize team-building activities through educational outreach
  • Matching Gift Programs: Double the impact of employee donations to education
  • Sponsorship Opportunities: Support specific programs or learning centers
  • In-Kind Donations: Provide technology, supplies, or professional services
  • Cause Marketing: Align your brand with educational equity initiatives
